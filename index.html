Still Have Questions?

Below are some answers to frequently asked questions that you may find useful.

What time will the signals be sent?

Whether Free or Premium, signals are usually sent between 8:30 am - 11:00 am.

What pairs are traded?

Only EUR / USD

What is your average monthly percentage in profit?

Over the last 6 months, I've averaged 12.5%

Can I pass a prop firm challenge with your signals?

YES with proper risk management. Many people have used my signals to pass their challenges.

What size stop loss will be used?

It'll depend on the trade, but almost always either 8 or 10 pip stop loss.

At what take profit should I close my position?

I recommend closing at least at a 1:2 (Take Profit 2)

Should I trade my own capitol or use a funding company?

I recommend using a funding company. That way you can make more money based off the same % made in the group.

How many trades do you send out each week?

On average, 2-5 trades per week.

Is it just a signal group, or a learn & earn?

Scalp Trading Lab is definitely more than just a signal group. It is a true learn & earn experience where I provide my daily analysis before session, giving everyone my complete strategy.
